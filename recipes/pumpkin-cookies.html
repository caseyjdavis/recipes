Pumpkin Cookies

For the cookies

  • 4 sticks margarine
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 small can pumpkin pie filling
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp cinnamon

For the icing

  • 2 Tbsp butter melted
  • 2 Tbsp Half and Half
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cup powdered sugar
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, and salt; set aside.
  3. In a mixer, cream together the butter (that is at room temp) and sugar. Add pumpkin, eggs, and vanilla to mixture; and beat until creamy.
  4. Mix in dry ingredients and drop on cookie sheet using a cookie scoop.
  5. Bake for 12-13 minutes in the preheated oven or until cookies are slightly browned on top.
  6. Cool cookies on a baking rack.
  1. Combine the melted butter, half and half, and vanilla. Then add enough powdered sugar to make it easy to spread but not too runny that it runs off the cookie.
  2. Spoon the icing on top of the cooled pumpkin cookies.
